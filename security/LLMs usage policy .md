## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to ensure that LLMs (eg. ChatGPT, Anthropic) and tools using LLMs (eg. **CursorAI**, **GitHub Copilot**, **Notion AI, Miro AI**) are used in a confidential, responsible and ethical manner.

## Background

**ChatGPT** and other similar language models are powerful tools that can enhance productivity and efficiency in various areas of our organization. However, their usage also poses potential risks and challenges that must be addressed to ensure responsible and ethical use. This policy outlines the guidelines for the proper usage of LLMs (eg. ChatGPT, Anthropic) and tools using LLMs (eg. **Cursor**, **GitHub Copilot**, **Notion AI, Miro AI**) within our organization.

### Approved AI systems

Employees may use only AI systems that have been explicitly approved by Manychat and are included in the official business systems inventory.

The use of external or public AI services (including personal accounts of ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Midjourney, or similar tools) for work-related purposes is prohibited unless explicitly approved by the Security team.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and other third-parties who use LLMs and similar tools on behalf of Manychat.

Only company-approved AI systems may be used for work-related purposes.

The use of any external or public AI systems is prohibited unless explicitly approved and included in Manychat’s business systems inventory.

## Policy

**1. Purpose of Usage:** ChatGPT and similar tools should only be used for legitimate business purposes that are aligned with our organizational goals and values. Usage for unauthorized purposes is strictly prohibited (eg. how to assemble a bomb from a grocery store goods).

The usage of ChatGPT or similar tools should be inline with [Manychat’s Core Values](https://www.notion.so/Mission-Values-6d6ddfee93854754a10e97b633dd6f36?pvs=21).

**2. Confidentiality:** 

Confidential or sensitive information must not be entered into any AI system unless the system is explicitly approved for handling such data. 

At present, Confidential data may only be processed in Manychat’s corporate ChatGPT environment and only if such usage is explicitly approved by the Security team based on the data category and business purpose. 

Definitions of Confidential information are outlined in the [Data Classification Policy](https://www.notion.so/Data-Classification-Policy-2bfb12e9aa1a809aa48bd02294c10582?pvs=21) (POL-IS-02)

Still, the following information shouldn't be disclosed to any AI systems:

- credit card numbers
- customers’ or subscribers’ personal data
- national IDs, passports or other government document ID numbers
- passwords or other secret keys.

**3. Data Filtration:** Automated use of AI systems (including API integrations, automated uploads, data pipelines, or background processing) requires prior approval from the Security team.

If you’re planning to use ChatGPT or other similar tools APIs in automated integration make sure to filter all passing content in order to meet confidentiality requirements. For example:

- Replace all digits with zeros or other random digits
- Replace or erase customers’ or subscribers’ personal data (names, addresses, social network links, etc.)
- Replace email addresses with some generic emails (eg. [mail@mail.com](mailto:mail@mail.com))
- Replace hashtags and links like @person with some generic information (eg. `#hashtag` and `@atlink`).

**4. Data Privacy:** Users must comply with all relevant data privacy laws and regulations when using LLMs. They must ensure that any data provided to the tool is collected and used in a lawful and ethical manner.

**5. Accuracy and Quality:** Users must ensure that any output provided by LLMs is accurate and of high quality. They must verify the accuracy and quality of any output generated by the tool before using it for business purposes.

**6. Human Oversight:** While LLMs can automate certain tasks, they should not replace human oversight entirely. Users must ensure that they are adequately monitoring and reviewing the outputs generated by the tool to ensure their accuracy and appropriateness.

**7. Ethical Use:** Users must use LLMs in an ethical and responsible manner. They must avoid using the tool to generate content that is discriminatory, offensive, or otherwise inappropriate.

**8. Bias Awareness:** Users must be aware of potential biases that may exist in the tool's algorithms and outputs, and take steps to mitigate these biases where possible.

## Compliance and Enforcement

Any violations of this policy will be subject to appropriate disciplinary action, which may include revocation of access to the tool, or other sanctions deemed appropriate by the organization. Any suspected data exposure, misuse, or malfunction involving AI systems must be reported immediately to the Security team via [security@manychat.com](mailto:security@manychat.com) or the #security Slack channel.

## Review and Update

This policy will be reviewed periodically and updated as necessary to ensure its continued relevance and effectiveness.
